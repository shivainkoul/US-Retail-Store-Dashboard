<H1>US RETAIL STORE DASHBOARD</H1>

<BR><H2>1. Project Title</H2>

🏬 US Retail Store Dashboard


<H2>2. Short Description</H2>

A Tableau dashboard for analyzing US retail sales data. It provides insights into sales, profit, cost, customer segments, and regional performance using interactive visuals.


<H2>3. Tech Stack</H2>

• 📊 Tableau Desktop  
• 📂 Data Cleaning (Excel / CSV)  
• 📈 Calculated Fields & Aggregations  
• 🔗 Data Blending / Relationships  
• 📁 .twbx / .png  


<H2>4. Data Source</H2>

Source: US Retail Sales Dataset  

<br>Includes:

• Order Date  
• Category & Sub-Category  
• Sales, Profit, Cost  
• Quantity  
• Customer Segment  
• State / Region  


<H2>5. Features / Highlights</H2>

🔎 Business Problem

Retail data is complex and large, making it difficult to track KPIs, identify trends, and compare performance across regions and categories.


<H2>🎯 Goal of the Dashboard</H2>

• Monitor sales, profit, cost, and quantity  
• Identify top-performing categories and regions  
• Track monthly sales trends  
• Analyze customer segments  
• Support business decision-making  


<H2>📊 Key Visuals</H2>

📌 KPIs  
• Total Sales: 5,149K  
• Total Profit: 1,492K  
• Total Cost: 3,657K  
• Total Quantity: 75K  

<br>📈 Sales Trend (Line Chart)  
• Monthly sales trend with overall direction  

<br>🏷️ Sales by Sub-Category (Bar Chart)  
• Accessories, Binders, Chairs, Phones, Storage, Tables  

<br>🌍 Sales by State (Map)  
• Highlights performance in states like California, Texas, New York  

<br>📊 Sales by Region (Bar Chart)  
• Central, East, South, West comparison  

<br>👥 Segment Distribution (Pie Chart)  
• Consumer, Corporate, Home Office  

<br>📊 Profit Heatmap  
• Profit distribution across sub-categories and regions  

<br>📊 Category-wise Comparison  
• Sales vs Profit across sub-categories  

<br>🎛️ Filters  
• State  
• Sub-Category  
• Segment  
• Region  


<H2>🚀 Business Impact</H2>

• Identifies top-performing regions and products  
• Helps track profitability  
• Supports targeted strategy planning  
• Highlights trends and growth opportunities
